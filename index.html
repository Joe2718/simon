Instructions

  1. Turn ON using the power switch.
  2. Press START
  3. After the tone and light sequence finishes, click the coorsponding colors to repeat it.
    SIMON will change to a light color when it's your turn.
  4. Keyboard may also be used instead of clicking.
    • Up Right Down and Left arrows.
    • 8, 6, 2, and 4 on the NUMpad
    • W A S D
If you make a mistake, don't worry. The pattern will repeat itself and you'll have another try.

STRICT: If strict is selected, game will END if you make a mistake.

Special Note: If game has been played once and ended, clicking any color will play the tone.
